Sunshine Kindergarten For early education

Welcome to Su nshine Kindergarten! We are excited to have you and your child join our classroom. Let's have a fun and educational year together!

Our goals

Our goal is to establish an educational institution that aims to build a strong educational base for children that enables them to start their educational journey effectively and creatively. The institution aims to discover and develop talents in children

Explore Our Courses

King of Reading Activity

Contains many stories and educational books dedicated to children to develop reading skills.

Learning and Playing Activity

Here, we work on developing communication skills through educational games that are useful for building the child’s social skills

The Little Artist Activity

This class aims to develop the child's skills in learning and expression by allowing them to expand their imagination through drawing and coloring.

Friends of letters activities

he activity aims to start the journey of learning letters, their spelling, and how to draw them, and to help the child form words that help in expression and description.

Find My Talents Activity

This class aims to help children find their talents and contribute to their development.
